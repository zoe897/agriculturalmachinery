# TTS350 - TTS404 Series Compact Tractor

This series is specifically designed for small farms, greenhouses, and orchards. Its compact size and flexible steering make it the best choice for narrow spaces.

## Key Specifications
| Model | TTS350 | TTS354 | TTS400 | TTS404 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Drive Type** | 2WD | 4WD | 2WD | 4WD |
| **Engine Power** | 25.8 kW | 25.8 kW | 29.5 kW | 29.5 kW |
| **PTO Speed** | 540/720 r/min | 540/720 r/min | 540/720 r/min | 540/720 r/min |
| **Front Tread** | 1123mm | 1175mm | 1127mm | 1215mm |

## Performance Advantages
*   **Narrow Tread Option:** Optional 960mm wheel tread for special narrow-row crops.
*   **Transmission:** 8+2 gear shift (Optional 10+2) provides reasonable speed matching.
*   **Easy Operation:** Equipped with a hydraulic steering system for light and flexible handling.
*   **High Adaptability:** High ground clearance ensuring excellent trafficability in various terrains.
