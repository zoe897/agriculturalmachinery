# Disc Harrow Series

Based on **16 years of expertise** in agricultural machinery, we offer a comprehensive range of disc harrows designed for different soil types and tractor horsepowers. From light-duty finishing to heavy-duty primary tillage, our harrows ensure optimal soil structure.

### Our Product Range Includes:
* **1BJX Series:** Medium-duty offset disc harrows, perfect for loosening and breaking up clods after plowing.
* **1BZ Series:** Hydraulic heavy-duty offset disc harrows for large-scale operations and tough soil conditions.
* **1BQX Series:** Light-duty disc harrows for garden and small farm seedbed preparation.

---

### Core Advantages
* **High-Strength Discs:** Equipped with 65Mn or Boron steel discs for maximum wear resistance.
* **Hydraulic Control:** Easy transport and depth adjustment from the tractor seat (available on 1BZ series).
* **Consolidation Friendly:** Optimized designs that allow for efficient LCL (Less than Container Load) shipping along with other spare parts.

---

### Selection Guide
Choose the right series based on your tractor's horsepower:
1.  **40-80 HP:** Recommended 1BJX Series.
2.  **90-160+ HP:** Recommended 1BZ Heavy-Duty Series.
