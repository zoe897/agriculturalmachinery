# TTB904 - TTB1204 Flagship Series

Our most powerful series designed for large-scale farmland. With a 12+12 shuttle shift transmission and professional-grade hydraulics, it handles the toughest tasks with ease.

## Key Specifications
| Model | TTB904 | TTB1004 | TTB1204 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engine Power** | 66.2 kW | 73.5 kW | 88.2 kW |
| **Transmission** | 12F + 12R | 12F + 12R | 12F + 12R |
| **Max Traction** | 19.7 kN | >19.7 kN | >19.7 kN |
| **Front Tread** | 1350-1550mm| 1350-1550mm| 1350-1550mm|

## Performance Advantages
*   **12+12 Shuttle Shift:** Standard shuttle shift provides a wide speed range and smooth transitions.
*   **Heavy Duty Lifter:** Strong pressure lifter for heavy-duty plows and seeders.
*   **Side Shifting:** Compact structure with side-mounted gear levers for a spacious operator platform.
*   **Adjustable Tread:** Wide range of adjustable front and rear wheels for different crop requirements.
