# TTE500 - TTE604 Advanced Series

Featuring a new streamlined hood design, this series focuses on operator comfort and high-efficiency power output with a compact structural layout.

## Key Specifications
| Model | TTE500 | TTE504 | TTE600 | TTE604 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engine Power** | 36.8 kW | 36.8 kW | 44.2 kW | 44.2 kW |
| **Max Lifter Force**| 8.3 kN | 8.3 kN | 9.95 kN | 9.95 kN |
| **Transmission** | 8+2 Gear | 8+2 Gear | 8+2 Gear | 8+2 Gear |

## Performance Advantages
*   **Shuttle Shift:** Optional shuttle shift with a wider speed range for frequent forward/reverse tasks.
*   **Ergonomic Design:** Side-shifting gears and an elegant streamlined hood.
*   **Small Turning Radius:** Shortened wheelbase makes it flexible for turning in limited field spaces.
*   **Strong Pressure Lifter:** Adopted lifter with strong pressure for heavy implements.
