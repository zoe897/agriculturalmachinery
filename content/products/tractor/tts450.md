# TTS450 - TTS504 Series Utility Tractor

A perfect balance of power and size, the TTS series is a workhorse for medium-sized agricultural tasks, providing strong traction and reliable performance.

## Key Specifications
| Model | TTS450 | TTS454 | TTS500 | TTS504 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engine Power** | 33.1 kW | 33.1 kW | 36.8 kW | 36.8 kW |
| **Max Traction** | 8.3 kN | 9.0 kN | 8.5 kN | 9.2 kN |
| **PTO Speed** | 750/1000 | 540/1000 | 750/1000 | 540/1000 |
| **Min. Weight** | 1474 kg | 1630 kg | 1485 kg | 1710 kg |

## Performance Advantages
*   **Strong Power:** Equipped with famous Chinese engines (Laidong/Xichai) with large torque reserve.
*   **Efficient Operation:** Optional dual-stage clutch to improve work efficiency during implement usage.
*   **Hydraulic Output:** Optional multi-channel hydraulic output to meet different agricultural needs.
