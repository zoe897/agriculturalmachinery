# TTE700 - TTE804 Performance Series

Built for high-intensity agricultural operations, these models offer massive traction and reliable lifter force for larger implements.

## Key Specifications
| Model | TTE700 | TTE704 | TTE800 | TTE804 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engine Power** | 51.5 kW | 51.5 kW | 58.8 kW | 58.8 kW |
| **Max Traction** | 9.3 kN | 15.69 kN | 9.3 kN | 18.7 kN |
| **Max Lifter Force**| 12.36 kN | 12.36 kN | 14.11 kN | 14.11 kN |

## Performance Advantages
*   **High Efficiency:** 8+2 or optional shuttle shift transmission for optimized speed matching.
*   **Stepless Regulation:** Rear wheel tread is steplessly adjustable to fit various row spacing.
*   **Strong Power:** Matching famous engines with China III emission standards.
